\# Code of Conduct



\## Our Pledge



We, as contributors, maintainers, and community members of \*\*Aksara Nusantara\*\*, are committed to making participation in our project a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.



Our mission is not only to build software, but also to help preserve, study, and promote the rich writing systems and cultural heritage of the Indonesian archipelago. We believe that collaboration, curiosity, kindness, and mutual respect are essential to achieving this mission.



\---



\## Our Standards



Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:



\- Being respectful and considerate in discussions.

\- Welcoming contributors regardless of experience level.

\- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.

\- Supporting newcomers and helping others learn.

\- Respecting different perspectives, cultures, and academic opinions.

\- Using reliable references when discussing linguistic, historical, or cultural topics.

\- Focusing on facts and evidence rather than personal attacks.

\- Recognizing that many traditional writing systems have regional variations and historical differences.



Examples of unacceptable behavior include:



\- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.

\- Personal attacks, insults, or offensive comments.

\- Trolling or deliberately provoking arguments.

\- Publishing private information without permission.

\- Spamming or disrupting discussions.

\- Repeatedly ignoring community guidelines after warnings.

\- Disrespecting cultural heritage or intentionally spreading misinformation.



\---



\## Respect for Cultural Heritage



Aksara Nusantara exists to preserve and promote the traditional writing systems of the Indonesian archipelago.



Discussions regarding scripts, orthography, historical manuscripts, transliteration, and linguistic interpretations are encouraged.



However, contributors are expected to:



\- Respect differing scholarly opinions.

\- Cite reliable sources whenever possible.

\- Discuss disagreements professionally.

\- Remember that historical research often contains multiple valid interpretations.



Healthy debate is welcome.



Disrespect is not.



\---



\## Maintainer Responsibilities



Project maintainers are responsible for:



\- Clarifying community standards.

\- Reviewing reports fairly.

\- Taking appropriate action when necessary.

\- Maintaining a welcoming environment for all contributors.



Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.



\---



\## Scope



This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces related to Aksara Nusantara, including but not limited to:



\- GitHub Issues

\- Pull Requests

\- Discussions

\- Documentation

\- Community chat platforms

\- Public events representing the project



\---



\## Reporting



If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through GitHub or another official communication channel.



All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed fairly.



\---



\## Enforcement



Community leaders will review reported incidents and determine appropriate responses.



Depending on the severity of the violation, actions may include:



1\. Friendly reminder.

2\. Official warning.

3\. Temporary restriction.

4\. Permanent removal from the community.



The goal of enforcement is to protect the community, encourage learning, and maintain a respectful environment.
