\# Aksara Nusantara Manifesto



> Preserving the scripts of the past,

> empowering the generations of the future.



\---



\## Why We Exist



Indonesia is home to one of the richest writing traditions in the world.



For centuries, dozens of indigenous scripts have been used to record knowledge, literature, religion, science, history, and culture.



Many of these scripts are now rarely taught, difficult to access, or slowly disappearing from everyday life.



Aksara Nusantara exists to help preserve this priceless heritage through technology, education, and open collaboration.



\---



\## Our Vision



We envision a future where every traditional script of the Indonesian archipelago can be:



\- Learned freely

\- Studied openly

\- Digitally preserved

\- Shared globally

\- Improved collaboratively



Technology should not replace cultural heritage.



Technology should help preserve it.



\---



\## Our Mission



Our mission is to build an open-source ecosystem for learning, researching, documenting, and preserving the traditional writing systems of Nusantara.



This includes:



\- Educational applications

\- Digital fonts

\- Learning materials

\- Transliteration tools

\- Unicode resources

\- Historical references

\- Community collaboration



\---



\## Open Source First



Knowledge belongs to everyone.



We believe that cultural preservation becomes stronger when knowledge is shared instead of hidden.



Aksara Nusantara is developed as an open-source project because collaboration creates better software, better research, and stronger communities.



Everyone is welcome to contribute.



\---



\## Respect History



Traditional scripts have evolved over centuries.



Regional variations, historical manuscripts, and scholarly interpretations are all valuable.



We encourage respectful discussion supported by reliable references.



Healthy debate advances knowledge.



Respect preserves community.



\---



\## Education Before Perfection



Learning is a journey.



Our goal is not merely to create perfect software, but to help more people discover and appreciate the beauty of Nusantara's writing systems.



Every learner matters.



Every contribution matters.



\---



\## Community Over Ownership



This project is bigger than any individual.



We believe the best ideas come from collaboration.



Whether you are:



\- a student,

\- a teacher,

\- a historian,

\- a linguist,

there is a place for you in this community.
